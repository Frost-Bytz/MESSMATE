**MESSMATE** is a smart meal delivery and management system designed for student hostels. It streamlines mess operations, enables direct hostel delivery, and ensures fair usage through a point-based ordering system and biometric integration.

## üöÄ Features

### 1. Hostel Direct Delivery
- Affordable, healthy meals delivered straight to hostels.
- Ideal for students unable to reach the mess physically.

### 2. Point-Based Ordering System
- Each student receives **1000 points/month**.
- **Mess Attendance (Fingerprint Authentication):**
  - No points deducted.
  - Tracks attendance and reduces available days.
- **Online Orders:**
  - Points are deducted from the monthly balance.
- **No Action:**
  - A small bonus is added to encourage consistent usage.
- Promotes balanced usage between mess and online ordering.

### 3. Anti-Misuse & Lock-in Logic
- Biometric + app integration prevents duplicate orders.
- Ensures fair and secure access to meals.

### 4. Part-Time Student Delivery
- Offers flexible earning opportunities for students.
- Ensures timely and reliable delivery within hostels.

### 5. Data Visualization Integration
- Connects to **Google Data Studio / Looker Studio** via Firebase or Google Sheets.
- Tracks:
  - Meal demand trends
  - Wastage reduction
  - Delivery performance
